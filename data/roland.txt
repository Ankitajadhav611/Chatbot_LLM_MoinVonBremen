Built in 1404 (its wooden predecessor was burned by the Archbishop's servants in 1366)
Size: 5.5 meters (from base to canopy 10.21 meters)
A chivalrous gentleman of his time
Medium length wavy hair
Clothing and equipment: leather jerkin, chainmail, belt, sword and shield
He owes his imperial coat of arms to forged imperial documents from the citizens
Distance between his pointed knees is: One Bremen ell (former unit of measurement)
He looks towards the cathedral, the former archbishop's seat
